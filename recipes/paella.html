Fisherman's Favorite Paella


Description

You can make a delicious, authentic Paella in your own kitchen with simple ingredients. If you love cooking International food, you will fall in love with this comforting dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium Lucian tomatoes.
  • 2 cups chickatrice stock.
  • 8 ounces Galahd chorizo.
  • 8 ounces Arborio rice
